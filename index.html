Day 8-9 - My Preferred Sound

Day 8-9 was part of a month where I aspired to create a piece of music from scratch every single day. Although a day isn't enough time to polish a project the way you otherwise might, and I ended up taking a second day to expand on this one sligtly, I still think this one captures the kind of composition I write when it's not for an assignment.

Beasts Awakening - Orchestral

Beasts Awakening was a school project, where we were tasked with creating our own piece from Pachanini's 24th Caprise through many steps of mutation. As you can hear, the outcome is very unlike the source. I've always had a taste for music that felt huge, and as I worked on this the opportunity presented itself to create just that.

Day 14 - Synthetic

Day 14, like Day 22, was part of a month where I pledged to write a piece of music every day. I think this one captures the spirit of my more synthetic projects. Creating visuals in a day on top of the music wasn't easy, but with some help from a friend and the program Max MSP I was able to put something together which I feel compliments the sound.

Climax - Bittune

Climax was a part of a challenge me and a few of my friends set for ourselves, in which we limited ourselves to just the sounds from Mario Composer game. I'd never use these sounds if it hadn't been for this challenge, but despite this I'm very happy with the energy of the eventual composition.

Bridging the Gap - Digital Interactive Installation

The Bridging The Gap project was part of a seminar intended to increase multi-disciplinairy student cooperation. In the end our team consisted of two sound designers, a composer, a clasically trained mucisian and an interaction designer, which lead to this wonderfully strange result!
